The First Mouse Was Wooden 🖱️

The very first computer mouse was made out of wood and had just one button. It was created in the 1960s and looked more like a block of cheese than the sleek designs we see today.

Bugs Were Literal 🪲

The term “computer bug” comes from a real bug — a moth — that got stuck in a computer back in 1947. It caused a malfunction, and the team taped it into a logbook as the "first actual case of a bug being found."

Your Phone Is Stronger Than NASA’s 1969 Tech 🚀

The computer used to land Apollo 11 on the moon was way less powerful than a modern smartphone. Today’s phones have millions of times more memory and processing speed.
