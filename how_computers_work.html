How Computers Work

Inside a computer, everything is about talking with little electric signals! When you press a key or click your mouse, tiny switches inside the computer turn on and off really fast — way faster than blinking your eyes! All that magic helps the computer show you videos, games, and websites.

Magic Machines

It’s like a huge team of tiny workers inside your computer, all working together at super speed. That’s how even the craziest games and coolest apps are possible!
